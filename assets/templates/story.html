Let’s work together for our country


Agriyaan Technologies Private Limited, Group Company of Scyara which was founded on 29th October, 2020 with the motto to reach out to the farmers of India, who are contributing towards the country. The reach is through the development of state-of-the-art technologies and solutions for the problem. Agriculture being one of the most important sector for Indian economy, contributes to the GDP, also number of lives depend on it as one of the highest employment source as 60% of population depends on the same. Face of agriculture is gradually changing with the advancement of technology and creative mind-sets prevailing in the sector. Agriyaan took the initiative to work on the problems within India, which will lead to the path of independence in working development of our country and the promotion of “Make in India campaign”.
The working of the particular project, begin with four like-minded people came together with the same enthusiasm and the will to achieve the motto by using the formula to success. Furthermore, the dedication and interest which our highly effective employees shown became the major contribution towards the project. As, agriculture in the modern age is changing rapidly. Rising global population and shifting trade policies affect the pricing, supply chain, and delivery of food products. However, people are now more biased towards the organics and sustainable product which eventually help the farmer to protect their crops by lessoning the damage. Agriyaan Technologies is one of the fastest growing Agritech companies in the country evident by its recent recognition by Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation, Tata Trusts, IIT-Kanpur, CINI through Social Alpha program, and IARI-ZTMBPD.
